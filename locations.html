JUNGLE by Sturmfrei is a hostel in Goa known for its hospitality, party, music, open mic, vibe, community engagement, chill-out spaces, coworking space, etc.

JUNGLE by Sturmfrei, Goa

JUNGLE by Sturmfrei celebrates the thrilling life of all beach goers. Live music events, crazy parties, daily fun-filled activities and the open cafe-cum-bar makes the ambience of the hostel livelier. Anjuna, Vagator & Ozran being the nearby beaches, travelers can have a great experience of Goa's festive scenes and its old hippie culture too.


ZEN by Sturmfrei is a hostel in Gokarna, Karnataka known for its hospitality, vibe, community engagement, chill-out spaces, etc.

ZEN by Sturmfrei, Gokarna

ZEN by Sturmfrei is idyllically located on a hillock and allows for easy access to Gokarna's pristine beaches, breathtaking landscapes & pilgrimage sites all within a 5-km radius. The hostel is a 2-minute ride from the main bus stand and provides the perfect atmosphere to unwind, relax & escape the hustle & bustle of a city life.



ANTARA by Sturmfrei is a hostel in Jaipur, Rajasthan known for its hospitality, vibe, music, community engagement, chill-out spaces, etc

ANTARA by Sturmfrei, Jaipur

ANTARA by Sturmfrei situated in the heart of 'Pink City' creates a colourful vibe for all its guests. It is equidistant from the railway station and the renowned tourist attractions sites of Jaipur. The beautiful & spacious hostel terrace offers one of the best bird's-eye view of the historic city in all its cultural glory.



WHITE Forest by Sturmfrei is a hostel in Manali, Himachal Pradesh known for its hospitality, vibe, scenic views, community engagement, chill-out spaces, luxurious rooms and dorms, coworking space, etc.

WHITE FOREST by Sturmfrei, Manali

WHITE FOREST by Sturmfrei, which is nestled in the midst of apple & cherry plantations, offers stunning views of the snow-capped Himalayan mountains, lush green valleys & exquisite waterfalls. The hostel has a beautiful open cafe-cum-workspace area on the terrace overlooking the old quaint town of Manali & cozy yet affordable rooms for solo travelers & couples alike.
