About Us

Founded by alumni of IIM-I, Sturmfrei is a company with a vision to be part of life journey of Millennial/Gen Z by providing experiences and lifestyle solutions using physical and digital touch points. Into the 3rd year of its operations, Sturmfrei has created an impressive portfolio of physical and digital services with physical presence across Goa, Gokarna, Jaipur and Manali, and a mobile app "EVOY" as experiences aggregator.
